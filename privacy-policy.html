Who We Are

Truth in Real Estate® is a referral service operated by Kati Spaniak, a licensed real estate broker in Illinois and Florida with eXp Realty. Our website is truthinrealestate.com. When you use our website or submit a form, we collect certain information about you. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What Information We Collect

We collect information you give us directly, such as:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Your city, state, or zip code
  • Details about your home buying or selling situation
  • Any questions or messages you send us

We may also collect basic technical information automatically, such as your browser type and the pages you visit on our site, to help us understand how people use the site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Match you with a TruthCertified™ agent in your area
  • Follow up with you about your referral request
  • Send you helpful resources related to buying or selling a home
  • Respond to questions you submit through our site

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with agents until you have been introduced as part of the referral process.

How We Share Your Information

We only share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With agents you are matched with — once an introduction is made, the agent will receive your contact information to reach out to you directly.
  • With our team — our small internal team uses your information to coordinate your referral and follow up with you.
  • As required by law — we may disclose information if required to do so by law or legal process.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Our systems are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can opt out of email communications from us at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly. If you would like us to delete your information, please reach out and we will honor that request.
